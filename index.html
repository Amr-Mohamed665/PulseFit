Our Story

More Than Technology

In 2023, our founder faced a health scare that could have been prevented with earlier detection. That moment sparked a mission: to create a device that doesn't just track health — it actively protects it. PulseFit Pro X was born from the belief that everyone deserves access to intelligent, proactive healthcare insights. Three years of research, 47 prototypes, and partnerships with leading cardiologists later, we built the world's most intelligent smartwatch.

Our Mission

To democratize advanced health monitoring and empower individuals to take control of their wellness through AI-driven insights that were previously only available in clinical settings.

Certified Quality

MIL-STD-810H military-grade certification for durability. Medical device certification for health sensors. IP68 water resistance rating. CE and FCC compliant for global safety standards.
